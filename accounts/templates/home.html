About Us

Reimagining agriculture through nature and innovation

Ecopia is a biotech-driven sustainability startup focused on circular agriculture. We use black soldier fly technology to recycle organic waste into two powerful products: protein-rich animal feed and organic fertilizer. Our mission is to promote food security, reduce waste, and support regenerative farming across Africa.
